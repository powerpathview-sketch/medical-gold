About Us

Medical Gold Ltd. — Belgrade, Serbia

Ko smo mi

Medical Gold d.o.o. je kompanija sa sedistem u Beogradu, specijalizovana za prodaju hiperbaricnih komora i medicinske opreme na teritoriji Republike Srbije.

Posedujemo sve potrebne licence za promet medicinskih sredstava i lekova. Nasa misija je da klinikama, wellness centrima i zdravstvenim ustanovama u Srbiji obezbedimo pristup najsavremenijoj medicinskoj tehnologiji za hiperbaricnu kiseonicnu terapiju (HBO).

Saradnja sa OxyMed Poliklinikom

Medical Gold posluje u ekskluzivnoj saradnji sa OxyMed Poliklinikom d.o.o. iz Kopra, Slovenija. OxyMed je specijalizovana klinika za hiperbaricnu medicinu i otorinolaringologiju, ovlascena od strane Ministarstva zdravlja Republike Slovenije i Zdravniske zbornice Slovenije.

Mr. sci. med. Vladan Stanojevic, dr. med.

Na celu OxyMed Poliklinike je mr. sci. med. Vladan Stanojevic — specijalista fizikalne i rehabilitacione medicine sa fokusom na hiperbaricnu terapiju. Dr Stanojevic poseduje dugogodisnje iskustvo u primeni hiperbaricnog kiseonika (HBO terapija) za lecenje preko 120 medicinskih indikacija.

Zahvaljujuci ovoj saradnji, Medical Gold svojim klijentima u Srbiji obezbedujuje:

  • Pristup medicinski sertifikovanim hiperbaricnim komorama
  • Strucnu podrsku i konsultacije
  • Obuku za rukovanje opremom
  • Servis i odrzavanje uredjaja
  • Sertifikate i dokumentaciju potrebnu za zdravstvene ustanove

Nase licence

Medical Gold d.o.o. poseduje sve potrebne dozvole za promet medicinskih sredstava i lekova na teritoriji Republike Srbije. Svi uredjaji koje distribuiramo su medicinski sertifikovani i uskladjeni sa regulativom.

Who we are

Medical Gold Ltd. is a Belgrade-based company specializing in the distribution of hyperbaric chambers and medical equipment across the Republic of Serbia.

We hold all required licenses for the distribution of medical devices and pharmaceuticals. Our mission is to provide clinics, wellness centers, and healthcare facilities in Serbia with access to state-of-the-art hyperbaric oxygen therapy (HBO) technology.

Partnership with OxyMed Polyclinic

Medical Gold operates in exclusive partnership with OxyMed Polyclinic d.o.o. based in Koper, Slovenia. OxyMed is a specialized clinic for hyperbaric medicine and otolaryngology, authorized by the Slovenian Ministry of Health and the Medical Chamber of Slovenia.

Vladan Stanojevic, MSc, MD

OxyMed Polyclinic is led by Vladan Stanojevic, MSc, MD — a specialist in physical and rehabilitation medicine with a focus on hyperbaric therapy. Dr. Stanojevic has extensive experience in hyperbaric oxygen (HBO) therapy for the treatment of over 120 medical indications.

Through this partnership, Medical Gold provides its clients in Serbia with:

  • Access to medically certified hyperbaric chambers
  • Expert support and consultation
  • Equipment operation training
  • Service and maintenance
  • Certificates and documentation required for healthcare facilities

Our Licenses

Medical Gold Ltd. holds all required permits for the distribution of medical devices and pharmaceuticals in the Republic of Serbia. All devices we distribute are medically certified and compliant with regulations.
